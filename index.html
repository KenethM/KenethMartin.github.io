Howdy! I'm Kenneth Martin, I'm sure you already got that far. I'm from the Big Island, born and raised, and after living on a farm for most of my life, I was hanging up my hat and wanted to experience the flip side,working in a well-ventilated room with a desk surrounding me. I graduated from Hilo High School and pursued higher education in my hometown. I will be graduating in the spring semester of 2026 with a bachelor's in Computer Science. I hope to find work after graduation in areas where I'm able to work with and promote land and cultural conservation. As someone who loves to go out and enjoy nature, I want to do what I can to preserve it!

Outside of school and word, you'll find me in the water diving, scrambling up streams hunting for waterfalls, or jumping off rock faces into cold river pools.
